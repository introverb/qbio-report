Keywords

— the terms that define relevance for QUBIE News —
Keywords are used two ways: (1) to score every article's relevance (article score = sum of matched keyword weights), and (2) to query PubMed, arXiv, Europe PMC, Hacker News, and Bluesky server-side. Higher weight → higher rank in Breaking and elsewhere. Changes take effect on the next scraper run — no check-in needed.
